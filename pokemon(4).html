⚡ Energy System

Each deck has 10 Energy Cards. Start with 2 face-up (2⚡). Each turn: refresh spent cards, flip 1 new one. Rotate sideways to spend. Max 10⚡.

⚔ Turn Order

1
Deploy — spend Energy to play Pokémon to Back row
2
Advance — all non-Stadium Pokémon move 1 row forward
3
Combat — roll D20 per Pokémon. Nat 20 = ⚡ Poké Strike!
4
Towers fire — Gym Tower 1w · Pokémon Center 2w (once active)

🏰 Towers & Winning

  • Trainer (8w): 1 per lane. Fires 1w/round to nearest enemy. Falls at 8w.
  • Gym Leader (12w): Active when either Gym Tower falls. Fires 2w/round. Destroy it to WIN.

🃏 Card Types & Range

⚔ Physical
Back: no range · Front: hits Neutral · Neutral: hits all + tower
🔮 Special
Back: hits Neutral · Front: hits Neutral + tower · Neutral: hits all
🦅 Flying
Same as Physical. Can be targeted by ground units.
🏟 Stadium
Never moves. Fires 1 zone ahead. Never attacks towers.
🎒 Trainer
Instant spell — no D20. Discard after use.

📦 Deck Rules

  • 12 Pokémon/Trainer cards — max 2 copies of any card
  • 10 Energy Cards — mandatory
  • Requirements: 1 Tank (5+w Ground) · 1 Flying · 1 Special · 1 Stadium · 1 Trainer spell
